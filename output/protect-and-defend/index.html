Protect & defend

The strategies and actions taken to safeguard information systems and networks from cyber threats.

Description

In the context of cybersecurity, 'Protect & defend' refers to the proactive measures and defensive strategies employed by organizations to shield their IT infrastructure from a variety of cyber threats. This includes the implementation of security policies, deployment of firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and antivirus software, as well as regular updates and patch management to close vulnerabilities. Protecting an organization’s assets also involves security awareness training for employees to prevent social engineering attacks. The goal is not only to prevent breaches but also to mitigate the impact should a breach occur. Defending extends to incident response strategies that are activated when a security incident is detected, allowing for a swift and effective response to minimize damage. Overall, 'Protect & defend' encapsulates both the preventative and reactive aspects of cybersecurity that are essential in maintaining the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of information systems.

Examples

  • Implementing multi-factor authentication to secure access to sensitive systems.
  • Conducting regular vulnerability assessments and penetration testing to identify and remediate weaknesses.

Additional Information

  • Organizations should develop a comprehensive incident response plan as part of their defense strategy.
  • The cybersecurity landscape is constantly evolving, making continuous education and adaptation essential for effective protection.
